## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-18

### Added

- conversion of selected USD and EUR prices to PLN;
- support for common currency symbols, codes, decimal separators, and grouping separators;
- average exchange rates from the latest available NBP Table A;
- 12-hour rate cache with a fallback to data up to seven days old;
- isolated selection tooltip with click-to-copy;
- Firefox Manifest V3 package, automated tests, and reviewer documentation.
